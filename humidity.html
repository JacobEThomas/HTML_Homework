Latitude vs Humidity

This plot has Latitude Plotted on the X-Axis, and Humidity Plotted on the Y-Axis.

Weather parameters were pulled in realtime usine the OpenWeatherAPI.

The relationship between humidity and latitude is less clear. It appears that the data clusters around high aread of humidity both close to the qeuator - 0 deg lattitude - and close to the pole - 180 deg lat. There are more frequent measurements of lower humidity at the middle lattitudes.
